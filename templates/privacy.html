Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password. As you use the Service, we also collect the expense records you log — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — as well as basic usage data that helps us keep the Service running smoothly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve Spendly's core functionality: letting you log expenses, view spending summaries, and manage your account. We do not use your expense data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in our database and retained for as long as your account remains active. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, but no method of storage or transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal expense data with third-party services. Where the Service relies on external providers for functionality such as hosting or email delivery, those providers only receive the minimum data necessary to perform their function and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.example.
